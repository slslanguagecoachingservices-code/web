Meet the team

Meet the team at Scholarly Language Services (SLS), a diverse group of experts dedicated to supporting your language journey, from exam preparation (IELTS, CELPIP, PET) to professional language development. Our team is passionate about helping you achieve your language goals and unlock your full potential.

Scholastica Alibo Mfeseer – Founder & Head Language Coach/Instructor

As the visionary behind Scholarly Language Services, Scholastica Alibo brings over a decade of experience in language education and communication coaching. With a deep passion for linguistic excellence and personalized learning, she leads the team in delivering tailored language instruction that meets the highest academic and professional standards. Scholastica specializes in helping clients refine their academic writing, public speaking, and overall language fluency, fostering both confidence and competence in every learner.
